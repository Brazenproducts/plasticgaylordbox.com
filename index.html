What to Specify When Ordering

Ordering your first plastic Gaylord box is straightforward if you know what to ask for. The following checklist covers the key decisions your supplier will need to confirm before manufacturing:

Buyer's Specification Checklist

  • Interior dimensions (L × W × H in inches or mm)
  • Required static load capacity (lb or kg)
  • Forklift entry: 2-way or 4-way base pockets
  • Lid required: yes / no / optional
  • Food-grade PP resin required: yes / no
  • Custom print: yes / no — number of colours
  • Wall thickness: 4 mm standard / 6 mm heavy duty
  • Collapsible or rigid wall design
  • Operating temperature range (freezer / ambient / heat)
  • Required certifications (FDA, NSF, REACH, RoHS)
