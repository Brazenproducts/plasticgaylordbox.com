Buyer's Guide

What to Check Before Buying a Plastic Gaylord Box

Use this checklist when evaluating plastic gaylord boxes for your manufacturing or distribution operation.

✓

Wall Thickness

Minimum 4mm standard walls; 6mm in corner gussets and forklift pocket walls. Thinner walls bend under repeated loading and fail early.

✓

GMA Footprint (48×40")

Non-standard footprints cause compatibility issues with pallets, racking, and conveyor systems. Verify exterior dimensions match your infrastructure.

✓

Material Grade

Virgin HDPE or PP for food/pharma. Recycled content is acceptable for non-food industrial applications and reduces cost 15–25%.

✓

Static vs Dynamic Load Rating

Static rating is for stored loads. Dynamic (forklift) rating is typically 60–70% of static — always specify the moving load in your application.

✓

Stack Height (loaded)

Most industrial gaylords stack 2–3 high when loaded. Verify rack height clearance and floor stack limits with your facilities team before ordering.

✓

Drain Plugs / Access Ports

For liquid-containing or wash-down applications, ensure drain port specifications match your floor drainage and cleaning protocol requirements.
