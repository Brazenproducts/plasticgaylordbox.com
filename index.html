What Is a Plastic Gaylord Box?

A Gaylord box is a large bulk container — typically pallet-sized — used to store and transport high volumes of product. Traditional Gaylords are made from cardboard, but plastic versions manufactured from PP corrugated offer dramatically better performance: waterproof, reusable, washable, and food-grade.

Elipacko manufactures PP corrugated Gaylord boxes for produce distributors, meat processors, seafood handlers, and industrial operations that move bulk product at scale. Order direct from the factory for the best pricing and full customization support.
